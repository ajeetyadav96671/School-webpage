Our Events

The legacy of celebrating the World Dance Day as VIBRATION was upheld once again at KDB Public School on 29th April 2023. It was an electrifying dancing event that portrayed ‘Versatile Indian Mythology - Story, Anecdote or Characters’. The dance performances by over ten schools embodied the spirit of unity, harmony, peace, and humanity, thereby transcending the borders of cultural diversity.

The day that recognizes the essence of a mother was celebrated in high bliss and merriment. This being one of those occasions that none of us can afford to miss was zealously enjoyed by one and all. Keeping this bond in mind, K.D.B. Public School organized a fun filled function for mothers on Friday 12th May 2023.

On May 15,2023 K DB Public School, felicitated the students who believed in the beauty of their dreams and have stepped forward towards their goal. More than 200 trophies and certificates were distributed among the students under the categories of academics, curricular activities, 100% attendance, good conduct and scholars for the three consecutive years, to motivate them and to perform better in the future.

ABOUT KDB

Since its establishment in 1950, K.D.B Public School has developed a reputation for excellence in all areas of student endeavour. A strong academic emphasis is complemented by opportunities in student leadership, creative activity sports and technology based education.

To impart education with multi-dimensional aspects and train young generation to develop them as valuable assets to the society.

KDB is an institution providing effective leadership, professional staff and an environment conducive to the holistic development of every child.
